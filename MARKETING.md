## Pricing Strategy

### Current: $3 One-Time
- ✅ Low friction (impulse buy territory)
- ✅ No subscription fatigue
- ❌ Need volume for revenue

### Alternative: Tiered Pricing
```
Basic: $3 - One-time cleanup
Pro: $9 - Unlimited cleanings (lifetime)
Business: $29 - 10 Macs (for teams)
```

### Alternative: Subscription
```
Monthly: $1.99/mo - Clean anytime
Annual: $19.99/yr - 2 months free
```

**Recommendation**: Start with $3 one-time, add Pro tier later
